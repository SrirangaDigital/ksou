Declares that all Government tanks are open to the Adikarnatakas
Order No. R. 2864-923-L.R.211-33-2, dated Bangalore, 15th December, 1933
Vol. 68, Issue no. 7, Part , December 21st, 1933, p.

It has been brought to the notice of Government that in some places the members of Adikarnataka Community have been prevented from taking water from Government tanks. Government are pleased to declare that all Government tanks are open to all the subjects of His Highness the maharaja, irrespective of caste or community for all legitimate purposes. Government Officers are directed to see that the instruction of any kind is offered to the members of the community while using these for any legitimate purpose.

S. ABDUL WAJID
Secretary to Government

Document No. (8)

General Secretariat Official Memorandum No. 4006-25-C.R.B. 25-32-1, dated 23rd December 1932

In Government Order No. 454-55-C.R.B. 1-28-3, dated 14th July 1928, it has been directed that when qualified candidates of the Adikarnataka community are available for employment as teachers in Adikarnataka Schools, they may be appointed direct for vacancies in such schools without advertisement.

All officers empowered to make appointments of teachers in such schools are informed that the above Government Order is not affected or modified in any respect by Government Order no. 64756624-C.R.B 14-31-2, dated 16th June 1932, directing that, for special vacancies, candidates of the Depressed Classes be given preference only when posses they qualifications approximately equal to those of the best qualified candidates among the applicants.

Such vacancies need not be advertised if qualified Adikarnataka candidates are available and in cases where it is necessary to advertise these, preference should be shown to Adikarnataka candidates.
