Document No. (3)

No. L. 5870-V.P. 9-31-87, dated 18th February 1932

Under Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, as amended from time to time, it is hereby notified that the property described hereunder is required for a public purpose, viz., for the construction of the Adikarnataka School building.

Under Section 5-A, it is directed that any person interested in the said property may, within 30 days after the issue of this notification, prefer objections, if any thereto, to the Assistant Commissioner in charge of Dodballapur sub-Division, in writing, after which no objections will be heard.

A plan of the property will be available for inspection in the Dodballapur Sub-Division Office, Bangalore.

Document No. (4)

No. L. 5712-V.P. 14-31-62, dated 16th February 1932

Under section 6 of the Land Acquisition Regulation, No. VII of 1894, it is hereby declared that the land described hereunder is required for a public purpose, viz., for the extension of the Adikarnatakas lane in Allampura Village, Chikmagalur Taluk and under sections 7 and 3 ( c) of the said Regulation, the Assistant Commissioner in charge of District Treasury, Chikmagalur is authorized to take order for the acquisition of the said lands.

Document No. (5)

No. L. 5292-V.P. 16-31-135, dated 28th January 1932

Under Section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, as amended from time to time, it is hereby notified that the property described hereunder is required for a public purpose, viz., extending Adikarnataka hatti at Boda village, Davangere Taluk..

Under Section 5-A, it is directed that any person interested in the said property may, within 30 days after the issue of this notification, prefer objections, if any thereto, to the Assistant Commissioner , Davangere sub-Division, in writing, after which no objections will be heard.

A plan of the property will be available for inspection in the Office of the Assistant Commissioner, Davangere Sub-Division.
