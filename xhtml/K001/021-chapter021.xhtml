Document No. (1)

Notifications

No. R. 4609-L.R. 98-35-63, dated Bangalore, 31st January 1936

(February 6, 1936, Part IV)

The Government of His Highness the Maharaja of Mysore are pleased to direct that the following note be substituted for the note under Clause 7 to rule 43 of the rules under the Mysore Land Revenue Code inserted by Notification No. R. 772-L.R. 8-31-62, dated the 6th August 1932:-

"Depressed classes in the above paras include Adikarnatakas, Adidravidas, Banjaras, Voddars, Korachars, Koramars, Hill tribes, Animists and members of the Pishari, Mochi and Iruliga communities"
