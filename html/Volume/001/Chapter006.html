1920 Part 2 - tables

TABLE 1 Showing details of Scholarship related documents for 1920

Sl. No. 1. Date/Volume details May 6, 1920, Part IV, pp. 351-383. Also publi shed on May 13th, Part IV 409-441. Place Extent and purpose Office of the Inspector General of Education, Mysore Remarks 1. Records the constitution of Ad Hoc Scholarship Committees (District and Taluk) for the administration of Backward and Depressed Class Scholarships during 1920-21. 2. Committees appointed for two consecutive years. 3. Various appendices in this document list out the functions and responsibilities of the Committees; provide application formats for different classes of scholarships (for both Backward and Depressed Classes); name members of district and taluk committees; provide tables showing distribution of scholarships to various caste groups in addition to providing districtwise data. 4. tables in the appendices also show the number of awardees from various Backward and Depressed Class caste groups. 5. the significance of this document is that the researcher gets an opportunity to study comparatively the distribution of education scholarships among Backward and Depressed classes. 6. Depressed Classes listed include Holeya, Madiga, Vadda, Korama, Lambani, Hill tribes and Animists.
2. September 9th, 1920, Part IV, pp. 974-5 Hassan District and Taluk Ad Hoc Scholarships Committees 1. Lists Grade IV and V Backward Class Scholarships as well as Grade A Depressed Class Scholarship Awardees. 2. 18 students awarded Depressed Class Scholarship. 3. All the students study in Panchama schools in various Taluks.
3. Sept. 30, 1920, Part IV, pp. 1015-17 Bangalore 1.Lists awardees receiving reallotted scholarships from various districts sent to Chairmen of District Ad Hoc Committes.
4. Sept. 30, 1920, Part IV,pp. 101821 Bangalore District and Taluk Scholarship Ad Hoc Committee 1. Shows the names of students receiving IV and V grade as well as A and B grade Depressed Class Scholarships for the year 1920-21 for Bangalore District. 2. Number of Scholarship awardees includes 33 Depressed Class students and 114 Backward Students.
5. Sept. 30, 1920, Part IV, pp. 1023-25. Shimoga District and Taluk Scholarship Ad Hoc Committee 1. Shows the names of students receiving IV and V grade as well as A and B grade Depressed Class Scholarships for the year 1920-21 for Bangalore District.
