Document No. (1)

No.L.4783-ML.17-43-26, dated 8th November 1943

Under Section 6 of the land Acquisition Act of 1894,as amended by the Land Acquisition Amendment Act No I of 1927 the Government of His Highness the Maharaja of Mysore declare that the lands measuring as detailed above, be the same a little more or less are needed for a public purpose to wit, for construction of houses to Sweepers; and under section 4 and 7 of the same Act, the Assistant Commissioner in Charge of Shimoga Sub- Division is appointed to perform the functions of a Deputy Commissioner under the Act and directed to take orders for the acquisition of the said land. Under sub-section (1) of the section 17 of the Act the Government further direct that possession of the said land may be taken on the expiry of fifteen days from the date of publication of the notice mentioned in Section 9(1) of the Act. A plan of the lands is kept in the office of the Assistant Commissioner in charge of Shimoga Sub- Division, and may be inspected at any time during office hours.

Under section 4 of the Land Acquisition Act, 1894, as amended from time to time, it is hereby notified that the property described hereunder is required for a public purpose, viz., for the formation of sites be granted to the Harijans.

Under Section 5-A, it is directed that any person interested in the said property may, within 30 days after the issue of this notification, prefer objections, if any thereto, to the Muncipal Commissioner, in writing, after which no objections will be heard.
