Document No. (1)

No. L. 5725-A. D. C. 3-52-60, dated 4th October 195211

Whereas it appears to His Highness the Maharaja of Mysore that the lands specified below are needed for a public purpose, to wit. Adikarnataka Colony.

Notice to that effect is hereby given to all whom it may concern in accordance with the provisions of sub-section (1) of section 4 of the Mysore Land Acquisition Act, 1894 (Mysore Act No. VII of 1894) and His Highness the Maharaja of Mysore hereby authorizes the Deputy Commissioner, Tumkur District, to exercise the powers conferred by Sub-section (2) of Section 4 of the said Act.

In exercise of the powers conferred by sub-section (4) of Section 17 of the said Act, His Highness the Maharaja of Mysore directs that in view of the urgency of the case, the provisions of Section 5-A of the said Act shall not apply to the acquisition of lands specified below.

Tumkur District, Tumkur Taluk, Kora Hobli, Kodihalli Village
