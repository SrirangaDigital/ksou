Document No. (1)

Excerpts from the Address of the Dewan of Mysore to the Dasara Representative Assembly, 23rd October 1912

Ethnographic Survey. 17 In connection with the Ethnographic Survey of the state, eight monographs were printed and published during the year, dealing with the Idiga, Madar, Golla, Uppara and other castes. One more monograph has since been issued, and two others are in the press.
